Democratic Vs. Republican Jobs

The US is politically polarized. Truck drivers are overwhelmingly republican, software engineers are majority democrat.

This is a game where you guess right, left, or down (mixed) on jobs in the American economy.

. All responses are recorded & data is publicly accessible. Play to learn about society's political divisons, and help us answer the question of: what do people think the divisons look like vs what they actually are.

Run your own study

This app allows the user to export/share their results at the end. That way if you want to run it on a specific group (your friends, or your classroom) you can ask people to go through it and fill out a form to share their data with you, so you can analyze it separately from random internet populations.

It's also open source/can run without a backend, so you can fork & tweak it. See setup.md on GitHub.
