Democratic Vs. Republican Jobs

The US is very polarized. Truck drivers are overwhelmingly republican, software engineers are majority democrat.

This is a game where you guess right, left, or down (mixed) on jobs in the American economy.

. All responses are recorded & dedicated to public domain. By playing (1) you check & update your mental model of America and (2) we collectively give shape to what we think this country looks like, vs what it actually is.
